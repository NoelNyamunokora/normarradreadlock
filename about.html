About Us

Our story, our passion, our commitment

Celebrating Identity Through Loc Artistry

Normarra Dreadlocks was born from a deep passion for natural hair and a vision to bring elite-level loc care to Mutare, Zimbabwe. For over five years, we've been more than just a salon—we've been a sanctuary where culture, identity, and artistry converge.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every loc tells a story, and every person deserves to wear their crown with pride. We recognized that in our community, there was a need for professional locticians who understood not just the technique, but the cultural significance and personal journey that comes with choosing to loc.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to hair health. We've seen too many beautiful locs damaged by improper techniques or harsh products. That's why we invested in extensive training, mastering both traditional methods passed down through generations and modern innovations in loc care. We use only natural, organic products that nourish the scalp and promote strong, healthy growth.

Our Mission

To provide Mutare with world-class loc care that honors tradition while embracing innovation, ensuring every client leaves feeling confident, beautiful, and proud of their natural hair journey.

Our Values

Excellence in technique, integrity in practice, respect for cultural heritage, and dedication to the health and satisfaction of every client who trusts us with their hair.

Our Promise

We promise to treat your locs with the care and expertise they deserve, using only methods that promote long-term health, never compromising quality for convenience.

Our Approach

01

Consultation First

Every client journey begins with a thorough consultation. We assess your hair type, discuss your goals, and create a personalized care plan that works for your lifestyle and vision.

02

Natural Products Only

We exclusively use organic, scalp-friendly products free from harsh chemicals. Your hair health is our priority, and we believe nature provides the best nourishment.

03

Expert Technique

Our locticians are trained in multiple methods—from traditional palm rolling to modern interlocking—ensuring we can provide the perfect technique for your unique hair needs.

04

Education & Support

We don't just style your locs; we educate you on proper home care, maintenance schedules, and techniques to keep your locs healthy between appointments.

Meet Our Team

Our skilled locticians are passionate professionals dedicated to the art and science of loc care. With years of combined experience and ongoing training in the latest techniques, we're committed to delivering exceptional results for every client.
